People with rashes and other sicknesses

Some sicknesses are particularly dangerous to pregnant women or their babies. Rubella (German measles) is one sickness that can cause serious birth defects in the baby, including deafness and heart problems. It can even cause death.

Pregnant women should stay away from people with sicknesses or rashes.
