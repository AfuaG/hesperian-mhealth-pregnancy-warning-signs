Chemicals and fumes

Strong chemicals used for cleaning, and poisons used to kill pests are especially dangerous to pregnant women.Chemicals can cause miscarriage, infertility, birth defects, cancer, and other illnesses.

Poisonous chemicals include: pesticides used in farming, pesticides used at home to kill bugs or rodents, strong cleaners and solvents, some kinds of glue and paint, gasoline, oil, and other fuels.
