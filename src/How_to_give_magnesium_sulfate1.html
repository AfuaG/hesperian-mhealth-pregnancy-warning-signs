Magnesium sulfate is used to stop seizures. It can cause the mother to have trouble breathing, especially if she is given too much. This medicine can save a woman's life, but only use it if you have been trained and you are on the way to a hospital or medical center.

Only use magnesium sulfate if her blood pressure is over 160/110 or she is having a seizure. Get medical help immediately if her breathing slows to less than 12 breaths a minute or if she has not urinated for 4 hours. Do not use on women with kidney problems
