What to do if labor has gone on too long

If her contractions are not coming every 2 or 3 minutes and lasting for a full minute, she may not be in good labor yet.

Encourage her to sleep. If she cannot sleep, ask her to roll her nipples and walk between contractions to help labor get stronger. Encourage her to drink and eat light foods.
