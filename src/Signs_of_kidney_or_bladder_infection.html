Signs of kidney or bladder infection

Bladder and kidney infections can be dangerous for the mother. They can also cause her to start labor too early if they are not treated right away.

Signs of bladder infection:

  • Constant feeling of needing to urinate, even after having urinated
  • Pain or burning while, or just after, urinating
  • Pain in the lower belly, behind the front of the pelvis

Signs of kidney infection:

  • Pain in the lower back, sometimes on the sides
  • Fever
  • Cloudy or bloody urine
  • Feeling very sick or weak
  • The woman may have signs of bladder infections as well
