Increased vaginal discharge

Discharge is the wetness all women have from thevagina. Pregnant women often have a a lot of discharge, especially near the end of pregnancy. It may be clear or yellowish. This is normal.

Changes in discharge can be a sign of an infection if the discharge is gray, green, lumpy, or has a bad smell, or if the vagina itches or burns. She should see a health worker if there are any of these signs.
