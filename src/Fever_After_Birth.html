Fever after birth

If the mother has a fever during the first weeks after the birth, she may have an infection of the womb. Other signs of womb infection are:

  • Chills
  • Pain and tenderness in the belly
  • Bad-smelling fluid from the vagina
Womb infection is very dangerous. It must be treated or the woman can become infertile or die.

Go to a medical center for antibiotics. If medical help is more than an hour away, give one of the following antibiotics on the way:

  • Give 2g ampicillin by mouth, 4 times a day for 7 to 10 days, AND
  • Give 400 to 500mg metronidazole by mouth 3 times a day, for 7 to 10 days.
