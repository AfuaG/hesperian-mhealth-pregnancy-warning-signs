Bleeding during the second half of pregnancy

Bleeding during the second half of pregnancy could mean the placenta is in the wrong place or has detached from the womb. Both are serious problems.

Bleeding during the second half of pregnancy is very dangerous. Get medical help to save the mother and the baby. Watch the woman for signs of shock.
