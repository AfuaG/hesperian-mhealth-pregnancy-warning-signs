Malaria

Malaria is an infection of the blood that causes chills and fever, spread by mosquito bites. Malaria is especially dangerous for pregnant women and their babies. A pregnant woman with malaria is more likely to have anemia, miscarriage, early birth, small baby, stillbirth (baby born dead), or to die.
