How to prevent malaria

Malaria is spread by mosquitoes. To prevent malaria, avoid mosquito bites.

  • Get rid of standing water and stay away from places where mosquitoes breed.
  • Use local remedies to keep mosquitoes away. Some people use citronella oil on their skin.
  • Wear long sleeves and long skirts or pants, or use mosquito repellant when mosquitoes are biting.
  • Sleep under treated bed nets or hang treated curtains in doors and windows.
